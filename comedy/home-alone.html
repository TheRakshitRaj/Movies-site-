Home Alone

Comedy | Family

Director: Chris Columbus

Cast: Macaulay Culkin, Joe Pesci, Daniel Stern, Catherine O'Hara

Release Year: 1990

Plot

Kevin McCallister, an 8-year-old boy, is accidentally left behind when his family goes on vacation. He must defend his house from two bumbling burglars using clever traps.

Highlights

  • Macaulay Culkin's iconic role as Kevin
  • Slapstick comedy and creative traps
