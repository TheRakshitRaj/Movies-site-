Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle

Adventure | Comedy | Fantasy

Director: Jake Kasdan

Cast: Dwayne Johnson, Kevin Hart, Jack Black, Karen Gillan, Nick Jonas

Release Year: 2017

Plot

Four high school students get sucked into the video game world of Jumanji, where they must survive dangerous challenges and work together to escape. Each takes on the avatar they chose, leading to hilarious and action-packed adventures in the jungle.

Highlights

  • Modern twist on the Jumanji story (board game turned into a video game)
  • Hilarious avatar personalities mismatch
  • Strong chemistry between Johnson, Hart, Black, and Gillan
