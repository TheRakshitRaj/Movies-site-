The Pursuit of Happyness

Biography | Drama

Director: Gabriele Muccino

Cast: Will Smith, Jaden Smith, Thandie Newton

Release Year: 2006

Plot

Based on the true story of Chris Gardner, a homeless salesman who struggles to create a better life for himself and his son through perseverance and an unpaid internship.

Highlights

  • Inspirational true story of determination
  • Strong emotional bond between father and son
  • Powerful performance by Will Smith
