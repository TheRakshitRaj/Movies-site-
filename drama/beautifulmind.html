A Beautiful Mind

Biography | Drama

Director: Ron Howard

Cast: Russell Crowe, Jennifer Connelly, Ed Harris

Release Year: 2001

Plot

The life of John Nash, a brilliant but asocial mathematician, and his struggles with schizophrenia while making groundbreaking contributions to game theory.

Highlights

  • Emotional biopic with a strong central performance
  • Insightful depiction of mental illness and genius
  • Academy Award-winning screenplay and acting
