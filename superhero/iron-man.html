Plot

Tony Stark, a billionaire inventor, builds an armored suit after being held captive in a cave and becomes the tech-powered superhero, Iron Man.

Highlights

  • Robert Downey Jr.'s iconic performance
  • Blend of humor, intelligence, and action

Critic Review

Fun Fact

The line “I am Iron Man” was improvised by Robert Downey Jr. and later became one of the MCU’s most iconic quotes.
