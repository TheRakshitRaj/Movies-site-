Spider-Man

Action | Adventure | Sci-Fi

Director: Sam Raimi

Cast: Tobey Maguire, Kirsten Dunst, Willem Dafoe, James Franco

Release Date: May 3, 2002

IMDb Rating: ⭐ 7.4 / 10

Plot

After being bitten by a genetically altered spider, teenager Peter Parker gains incredible powers and becomes the masked hero Spider-Man. As he learns that “with great power comes great responsibility,” he faces his first major foe — the ruthless Green Goblin.

Highlights

  • The origin story that defined a generation of superhero films
  • Willem Dafoe's chilling Green Goblin performance
  • Ground-breaking visual effects for early 2000s cinema

Critic Review

“A thrilling, heartfelt blockbuster that set the gold standard for comic-book movies.” — Roger Ebert

Fun Fact

Tobey Maguire performed many of his own stunts — including the cafeteria scene, which took 156 takes to get perfect!
