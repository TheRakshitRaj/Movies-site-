Edge of Tomorrow

Sci-Fi | Action

Director: Doug Liman

Cast: Tom Cruise, Emily Blunt, Bill Paxton

Release Year: 2014

Plot

A soldier finds himself reliving the same day every time he dies in battle against an alien invasion. With the help of a skilled warrior, he seeks to break the time loop and save humanity.

Highlights
