Troy

Action | Drama | History

Director: Wolfgang Petersen

Cast: Brad Pitt, Eric Bana, Orlando Bloom, Diane Kruger

Release Year: 2004

Plot

An adaptation of Homer's epic poem, "The Iliad", Troy follows the war sparked by the abduction of Helen, and the legendary conflict between Achilles and Hector.
