The Revenant

Adventure | Drama | Thriller

Director: Alejandro G. Iñárritu

Cast: Leonardo DiCaprio, Tom Hardy, Domhnall Gleeson, Will Poulter

Release Year: 2015

Plot

After being mauled by a bear and left for dead, frontiersman Hugh Glass embarks on a grueling journey through the frozen wilderness to seek vengeance on his betrayers.

Highlights

  • Leonardo DiCaprio's Oscar-winning performance
  • Stunning natural cinematography
