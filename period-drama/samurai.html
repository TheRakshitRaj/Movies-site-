The Last Samurai

Action | Drama | History

Director: Edward Zwick

Cast: Tom Cruise, Ken Watanabe, Billy Connolly

Release Year: 2003

Plot

A disillusioned American soldier finds redemption among Japan's samurai warriors as he learns their way of life and joins them in a fight to preserve their honor.

Highlights

  • Beautifully shot samurai culture
  • Emotional and redemptive storytelling
  • Powerful performances by Cruise and Watanabe
