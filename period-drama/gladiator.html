Gladiator

Action | Drama | History

Director: Ridley Scott

Cast: Russell Crowe, Joaquin Phoenix, Connie Nielsen, Oliver Reed

Release Year: 2000

Plot

A betrayed Roman general fights his way back as a gladiator to seek vengeance against the corrupt emperor who murdered his family and seized the throne.

Highlights
